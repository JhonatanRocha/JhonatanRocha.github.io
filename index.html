Magazine Luiza E-commerce Campaign

System do Manage E-commerce campaign and Pricing.

Magazine Luiza needed a new software to manage thier e-commerce strategy. They demanded Verx Consulting to develop a Web system with Java using JSF and it's Framework Vaadin, Hibernate 3, SQL Server, MySQL and Tomcat. The Software was implemented to manage their campaigns, pricing, shipping price (through micro regions creation), partnerships, brands, sale channels, payment condition, payment method and catalogs.

The Pricing Module is the most important feature because checks are made, through business rule, to prevent errors on the pricing setup and not allow products to be sold below the profit margin of the Company or above the limit values.
